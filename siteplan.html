Site Name

Utah Whitewater Rafting (short brand: UWR)

I chose this name because it is specific to Utah, clearly communicates rafting services, and is easy to remember for search and word of mouth.

  • Optional domain idea: utahwhitewater.co (domain purchase not required)
  • Logo concept: Monogram “UWR” with a wave shape inside a rounded badge.
UWR

Site Purpose

Provide a beginner-friendly hub for whitewater rafting in Utah, including: guided trip summaries and pricing, river difficulty overviews (Class I–V), safety guidelines, gear recommendations, photo highlights, and a simple contact/booking form for inquiries and group bookings.

  • Key Pages: Home, Trips, Rivers, Safety, FAQs, Contact
  • Audience: Families, students, and first-time rafters
  • Goal: Help visitors choose a trip and submit a lead via the contact form

Scenarios

  1. Which beginner-friendly trips are available near Provo and how much do they cost?
  2. What class (I–V) is each river section and what safety gear is required?
  3. How can I book a private group trip for 10 people next month?
  4. Are there age or weight requirements for kids?
